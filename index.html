About Me

My name is Steve and the past seven years I have been working as a Scientist in the biotechnology industry. There I discovered what software engineering could accomplish. Being naturally curious, I wanted to learn more about software engineering and development. After learning the basics on my own and having my first eureka moment solving an algorithm, I was hooked! I enrolled at Rithm, a full-stack immersive program, to begin my journey as a software engineer.

It allows music teachers to assign educational games to their students. You can also sign up as an individual. I worked in a team of six on a production code base to build features to support a new teacher admin portal.
